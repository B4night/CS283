Once upon a time, in the year 1963, John F. Kennedy, the 35th President of the United States, was assassinated on the streets of Dallas, Texas. This tragic event shocked the nation and the world, as the president was riding in an open-top car with his wife, Jacqueline Kennedy, and their entourage. As the motorcade passed through Dealey Plaza, a shot rang out, and the president slumped over in the back seat. It was a moment that would forever change the course of history.
